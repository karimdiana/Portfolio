About the Project

Developed for national television, this platform automates the creation of news content by generating video content and scripts from images. The system uses advanced AI to transform static images into dynamic video segments, complete with natural-sounding voiceovers and synchronized visuals.

Key Features:

  • Image-to-video generation for news segments
  • Automated script generation for news content
  • Real-time voice synthesis for natural narration
  • Seamless integration with broadcast systems
  • High-quality output meeting broadcast standards

Project Impact:

  • Reduced content creation time by 60%
  • Streamlined news production workflow
  • Maintained consistent quality across all generated content
  • Enabled rapid response to breaking news events

Technical Implementation:

  • Frontend: React, TypeScript
  • Backend: Node.js
  • AI: OpenAI's latest models
  • Video Processing: Custom pipeline for broadcast quality
