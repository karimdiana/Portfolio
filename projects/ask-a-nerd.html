About the Project

Ask-a-Dad is a question-and-answer app created during my business entrepreneurship class. The app connects users with real people — "Nerds" — who provide helpful, personal advice on everyday questions that don't always have easy answers online.

Key features of the platform include:

  • Personalized answers from verified experts
  • Affordable pay-per-question pricing with optional subscriptions
  • Warm, conversational tone that feels like advice from someone you trust
  • Follow-up questions, trust ratings, and optional tipping

Ask-a-Dad bridges the gap between impersonal search engines and expensive consultations by offering accessible, trustworthy answers to everyday problems — from fixing things around the house to understanding how insurance works.
